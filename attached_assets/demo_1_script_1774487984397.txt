We live in an era of constant disruption — economic shocks, job loss, health crises, geopolitical instability. Most people aren't prepared. Resilium changes that.
Resilium is an AI-powered personal resilience platform that analyzes who you are, where you live, and how you live — and turns that into a personalized action plan.
Click Get My Resilience Plan to begin.
The assessment takes about ten minutes and covers eleven dimensions of personal preparedness.
We start with mental resilience — ten short statements on how you handle stress, uncertainty, and crisis. Each one rated on a five-point scale.
These shape the psychological half of your report and unlock growth-oriented or stability-oriented recommendations based on your profile.
Then we capture where you are in the world — because geography affects your climate, economic, and political risk factors.
Income stability, financial runway, whether you have dependents — these feed directly into your financial and resources scores.
Your skills matter in a crisis. Practical abilities like coding, first aid, or speaking multiple languages all improve your resilience score.
Finally, you tell us what risks keep you up at night. Job automation, inflation, climate events — your priorities shape the action plan.
The AI builds your report in seconds.
Your overall resilience score is calculated across six weighted categories: financial stability, health, skills, mobility, psychological resilience, and resources.
The radar chart makes it immediately clear where your strengths are and where the gaps are. Here, financial and resources are the areas needing the most attention.
Below the chart, the AI writes a personalized risk profile summary — a plain-English interpretation of your situation, not a generic template.
Your mental resilience profile breaks down into six dimensions: stress tolerance, adaptability, learning agility, change management, emotional regulation, and social support. High scorers receive more ambitious, challenge-oriented recommendations.
The platform surfaces your most critical vulnerabilities, ranked by severity — from critical down to low risk.
Then comes the Action Checklist — a prioritized, interactive task list sorted by your weakest areas first. Check items off as you complete them and watch your progress toward four milestones: Momentum Building, Strong Foundation, Well Prepared, and Fully Resilient.
Finally, the Strategic Action Plan lays out concrete steps over three time horizons — what to do in the next thirty days, the next three months, and the next year. Each item is tagged by category so you know exactly what domain it addresses.
Running a new assessment and comparing it to an older one shows you a before-and-after overlay on the radar chart — so your progress is visible at a glance.
Behind the scenes, the admin dashboard gives operators a real-time view of platform health.
Charts show submission trends, demographic breakdowns, score distributions, and the most common risk concerns across the user base.
There's also a built-in AI UX testing suite — it runs autonomous AI personas through the full assessment flow and rates the quality of each generated report, so you can continuously improve the product without waiting for real user feedback.
