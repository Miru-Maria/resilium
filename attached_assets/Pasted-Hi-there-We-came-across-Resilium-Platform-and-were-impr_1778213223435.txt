We came across Resilium Platform and were impressed — here are some growth insights we put together:

### UX quick wins

-   The website's primary call to action, 'Free assessment in under 5 minutes', is clear and prominently displayed, aligning with best practices for conversion-focused design.
-   However, to further enhance user experience in 2026, implementing clear visual contrast for CTA buttons and strategic placement above the fold is crucial.
-   Given that most users access websites via mobile, ensuring a highly responsive and mobile-first design, with touch-friendly buttons and optimized images, is paramount for usability and search engine rankings.

### Marketing & positioning

-   The description effectively highlights the core value proposition: an objective resilience score across six dimensions and a personalized action plan.
-   In 2026, digital marketing emphasizes personalization and customer experience.
-   The 'personalized action plan' already addresses personalization, but further leveraging AI-driven personalization to adapt site experiences in real-time based on user intent signals would be a strong differentiator.

### Conversion ideas

-   The 'Free assessment in under 5 minutes' is a strong conversion hook, as clear, actionable CTAs are essential for higher engagement.
-   To optimize conversion rates, the website should focus on minimizing friction in the assessment process by simplifying forms and only asking for essential information.
-   Implementing A/B testing for different elements like button colors, microcopy, and placement is a best practice for identifying what resonates most with the audience and driving conversions.

### Differentiation

-   The platform differentiates itself by offering an 'objective resilience score across 6 dimensions' and a 'personalized AI action plan'.
-   To further strengthen its unique selling proposition (USP) in a competitive landscape, Resilium should emphasize the scientific basis or research backing of its 6-dimension model and AI.

### Technical

-   For optimal performance and search engine visibility in 2026, the website must prioritize fast page load speeds, measured by Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS).
-   Implementing mobile-first responsive design is crucial for search engine indexing and user experience.
