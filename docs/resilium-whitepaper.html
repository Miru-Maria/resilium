Executive Summary

Modern life is increasingly fragile. Supply chain disruptions, economic volatility, climate events, geopolitical instability, and health crises have converged to make personal preparedness a survival skill. Yet most individuals have no structured, data-driven framework for assessing or improving their resilience. Resilium fills that gap — quantifying readiness across six critical life dimensions and delivering a personalised, actionable improvement roadmap through validated psychological methodology, transparent deterministic scoring, and AI-generated planning. The platform is fully built, live, and operational on both web and mobile.

Why resilience planning is broken — and why it matters now.

Resilium addresses this directly — the platform's psychological assessment uses validated constructs from organisational and positive psychology to surface an individual's mental preparedness profile alongside their material one.

The Solution

A complete, multi-dimensional resilience assessment and planning platform.

2.1 What Resilium Delivers

Users complete a structured multi-dimensional assessment covering their financial position, physical health, psychological profile, skills portfolio, geographic context, and household logistics. A purpose-built AI engine processes these inputs and generates a personalised resilience report containing:

2.2 The Six Resilience Dimensions

Financial
25%

2.3 The Mental Resilience Assessment

A distinguishing feature of Resilium is its structured psychological evaluation. Unlike superficial stress questionnaires, the platform's 10-question Mental Resilience Assessment targets validated constructs from organisational and positive psychology. Each question uses a 1–5 behavioural frequency scale ("Rarely" to "Almost always") across six sub-dimensions: stress tolerance, adaptability, learning agility, change management, emotional regulation, and social support.

The resulting Mental Resilience Composite (MRC) directly informs the AI's planning strategy through pathway classification:

Growth Pathway
MRC ≥ 60
User has psychological headroom for ambitious, challenge-oriented goals. AI generates assertive, forward-looking action plans with longer horizons and opportunity-focused language.
Compensation Pathway
MRC < 60
User should focus on stabilisation before expansion. AI generates emotionally scaffolded plans with shorter milestones, reassurance-forward language, and confidence-building priorities.

The MRC also functions as a cross-area modifier: users on the compensation pathway receive a proportional dampening of their Financial and Skills scores, reflecting the documented compounding effect of psychological pressure on practical capability under crisis conditions.

2.4 Scenario Stress-Tests (Pro)

Pro subscribers access a scenario simulation suite that models the impact of specific disruptions on their current resilience profile. Each simulation provides per-dimension impact estimates, a recovery timeline, and scenario-specific action steps. Available scenarios: Job Loss, Natural Disaster, Health Crisis, Relocation. This transforms Resilium from a snapshot tool into a forward-planning instrument — users can answer "what if?" before a crisis, not after.

2.5 AI Engine

Resilium uses OpenAI's gpt-5.2 model via Replit's AI Integrations proxy for report narrative generation. Critically, the AI is not used for scoring — scoring is deterministic and auditable. The AI functions as a language and reasoning layer that synthesises multi-dimensional score data into coherent narrative, identifies non-obvious interaction effects between dimensions, and generates action plans calibrated to each user's geographic, economic, and psychological context.

Privacy, Ethics & Compliance

Privacy-first by design. GDPR-compliant. Fully auditable scoring.
Privacy by Design

All personal assessment data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. User data is never sold or shared with third parties. Anonymous assessments are automatically purged after 30 days. Full GDPR compliance: data export and complete account deletion available at any time from both web and mobile.

5.1 AI Transparency

Resilium's scoring methodology is deterministic and auditable — not a black box. The AI is used only for language generation, not scoring decisions. Users receive a full breakdown by dimension, enabling them to understand and scrutinise every aspect of their assessment result. This transparency is fundamental to user trust and regulatory compliance.

5.2 Data Minimisation

Only information strictly necessary for scoring is collected. No continuous location tracking, no background data collection, no third-party analytics SDKs with opaque data practices.

5.3 Consent Management

Versioned, timestamped consent is captured before any data collection on both web and mobile. Consent records are stored indefinitely as legal records, with a full admin audit trail for all GDPR-related actions. Users can request data export or full deletion at any time — from the mobile app's My Data screen or via the web profile.

5.4 No Medical or Financial Advice

Resilium explicitly does not provide medical, financial, or legal advice. All outputs are framed as educational self-assessment tools. Appropriate disclaimers are embedded throughout the user journey.
